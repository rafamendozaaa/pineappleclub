# Listing photos for the Parking Haus homepage

Upload your 6 parking photos into THIS folder.

How (GitHub, about 1 minute):
1. Branch selector at the top must say: claude/parking-hub-app-sf3Am
2. Open this `assets/listings/` folder on GitHub.
3. Click "Add file" -> "Upload files".
4. Drag your 6 images in, then "Commit changes" (directly to this branch).

Notes:
- Filenames don't matter (spot-1.jpg ... spot-6.jpg is fine) - I'll map each
  photo to the right card by looking at it.
- Square images are OK; they get cropped to 16:9 and compressed automatically.
